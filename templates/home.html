ABOUT

I'm 20 years old, from Brasília, and since childhood I took electronics apart to understand how they worked. I never stopped doing that — I just swapped screwdrivers for text editors.

HOW I STARTED

I started with robotics and circuit assembly. I ended up in design by mistake — I enrolled thinking it was IT — and graduated in two courses in the field. By the time I started Computer Science, design was already in my blood. Today I apply both sides to everything I do.

I am currently an intern at EasyPlan, where I develop and manage a sales system from scratch. At Ketsu, I work as a developer and designer — from briefing to delivery.

WHAT I DO

Frontend is where I feel at home. Backend is where I'm growing. Design is where it all begins. I don't separate these things — I believe the best code is the one that's also thought out visually.

I enjoy challenging projects. If someone wants to do something that has never been done, that's exactly where I want to be.

OBJECTIVE

To work in a company where what I build truly matters. Creating tools that make people's lives easier. One day, maybe, a big tech. For now, I'm paving the way.

KETSU

Ketsu is my startup. We offer everything from visual identity to systems development. It started as freelance, became a company. The goal is simple: to make people's lives easier with technology.
